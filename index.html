🔬 Dual-Pass AI Screening

Each article is reviewed twice independently by Gemini AI — matching the rigour of the command-line screen.py agent. Both passes must agree before a decision is finalised. Disagreements are flagged for human adjudication.

①Parse bibliographic files (BibTeX, RIS, PubMed/MEDLINE)
②Apply your inclusion/exclusion criteria (PICO format)
③Gemini reviews each article — twice, independently
④Agreed → Include/Exclude · Disagreed → Human review flag
